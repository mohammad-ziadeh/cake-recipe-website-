Delicious Chocolate Cake Recipe

Introduction Ingredients Steps

What is Chocolate Cake?

Chocolate cake is a rich, moist dessert made with cocoa powder, flour, eggs, butter, and sugar. It`s perfect for any occasion, especially for chocolate lover.


Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

Steps to Prepare Chocolate Cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the hot water and butter.
  4. Gradually add the eggs one at a time, beating until incorporated.
  5. Mix in the vegetable oil and vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients in a bowl, stirring constantly until just combined.
  7. Pour the batter into a greased 9x13 inch pan.
  8. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
